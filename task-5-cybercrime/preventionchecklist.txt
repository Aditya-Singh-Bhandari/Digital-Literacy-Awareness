Enable MFA: Always use Multi-Factor Authentication (like Google Authenticator or biometric locks) for all email and banking accounts.

Strong Passwords: Use unique, complex passwords of at least 12 characters; never reuse one password for multiple sites.

Regular Backups: Back up your academic files and projects to an external drive or secure cloud (like Google Drive) weekly.

Software Updates: Never "skip" or postpone OS or browser updates, as they contain critical patches against ransomware exploits.

UPI Security: Never enter your UPI PIN to receive money or scan QR codes from unknown buyers/sellers.

Transaction Alerts: Enable instant SMS/email alerts for every bank transaction to spot unauthorized activity immediately.

Phishing Awareness: Avoid clicking links in emails that create "artificial urgency," such as claiming your college account will be blocked.

Official Reporting: Report cybercrime immediately via the National Cyber Crime Portal (cybercrime.gov.in) or the 1930 Helpline.
